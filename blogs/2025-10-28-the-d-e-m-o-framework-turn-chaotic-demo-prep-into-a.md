Introduces a four-step D.E.M.O. framework (Diagnose, Engineer, Mobilize, Optimize) designed to turn chaotic demo preparation into a standardized operating system. It emphasizes how the system helps GTM teams reduce prep time and improve buyer engagement through stable, resettable environments and consistent narratives across sales teams.
